🚀 TimeSetu Guide

Master your digital habits with these powerful features

📊Understanding Your Dashboard

Your TimeSetu dashboard provides comprehensive insights into your browsing habits:

  • Total Time: See your daily or weekly browsing time at a glance
  • Quick Stats: Monitor sites visited, average session length, and productivity
  • Visual Charts: Switch between category and website views for detailed analysis
  • Top Sites: Identify which websites consume most of your time

🎯Setting and Tracking Goals

Take control of your digital habits by setting meaningful goals:

  • Click "Goals": Access your personal goal-setting dashboard
  • Set Category Limits: Define time limits for different website categories
  • Track Progress: Monitor your daily and weekly progress toward goals
  • Build Streaks: Maintain consistent habits with streak tracking

🔒Focus Mode & Website Blocking

Eliminate distractions during important work sessions:

  • Add Blocked Sites: Enter websites you want to block (e.g., facebook.com)
  • Enable Focus Mode: Toggle the switch to activate blocking
  • Set Timer: Optional - set a specific duration for your focus session
  • Stay Focused: Blocked sites will show a reminder to stay on task

📈Advanced Analytics

Dive deeper into your browsing patterns with advanced features:

  • Weekly Reports: Click "More" to access detailed weekly analytics
  • AI Voice Review: Get personalized insights about your digital habits
  • Export Data: Download your data for external analysis
  • Share Stats: Create shareable links to show your progress

💡Pro Tips for Success

Maximize your productivity with these expert recommendations:

  • Start Small: Begin with realistic goals and gradually increase them
  • Review Weekly: Check your weekly report every Sunday to plan ahead
  • Use Focus Mode: Block distracting sites during work or study sessions
  • Track Patterns: Notice which times of day you're most productive
  • Celebrate Progress: Acknowledge when you meet your goals
